# IMPORTANT DESIGN RULES

DO:

* create tension through atmosphere and pacing
* make players feel “I barely survived”
* use contrast between chaos and silence
* create readable gameplay situations
* prioritize gameplay feel
* design viral memorable moments

DO NOT:

* overload gameplay with mechanics
* make math overly difficult
* rely only on jumpscares
* create safe zones that feel fully safe
* explain horror directly through exposition
* create confusing UI
