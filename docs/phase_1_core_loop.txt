## PHASE 1 — CORE LOOP DESIGN

Tasks:

* describe the gameplay loop in detail
* create a 60-second vertical slice
* describe player emotions every 10 seconds
* explain the tension curve

Constraints:

* no unnecessary mechanics
* focus only on the strongest gameplay loop

Self-check:

* Is the gameplay understandable within 30 seconds?
* Does the player experience panic and relief?
* Is the loop replayable?
* Is the gameplay emotionally engaging without progression systems?
