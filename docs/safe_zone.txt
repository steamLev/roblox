# FAKE PLAYERS

Every few minutes, humanoid entities emerge from the forest.

They resemble normal Roblox players but behave unnaturally.

Behavior:

* standing near player
* pretending to explore
* delayed reactions
* slow movement
* occasionally copying player behavior

Uncanny traits:

* unnaturally wide toothy smile
* prolonged staring
* minimal interaction with the environment
* unnatural body movement

When fully revealed:
their lower jaw opens down to the chest.

The horror should come from:

* uncertainty
* observation
* paranoia
* behavioral abnormalities

NOT constant jumpscares.

---

# PSYCHOLOGICAL HORROR DETAILS

Sometimes before receiving a reward for solving a problem:
the player briefly sees the monster’s face with its jaw opened.

This should feel:

* uncertain
* fast
* almost subliminal

The player should question whether they really saw it.
