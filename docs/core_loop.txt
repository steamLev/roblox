# CORE GAMEPLAY LOOP

1. Player enters a glass dome
2. Player sees a math panel
3. Distant sounds:

   * drone buzzing
   * turret fire
   * impacts on glass
4. While solving the problem:

   * drones attack the dome
   * glass shakes
   * lights flicker
5. Heavy impacts trigger zero gravity
6. Objects and UI elements float
7. Player must solve the problem during chaos
8. Correct answer temporarily activates turret defense
9. Turrets destroy part of the drone swarm
10. Player feels temporary relief
11. Turrets eventually stop
12. Player hears growing drone buzzing
13. Looking upward reveals a massive swarm above the dome
14. Player must escape to the next dome
